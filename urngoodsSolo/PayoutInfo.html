Payout Information

At the start of this round, you have 40 POINTS and get an additional 60 POINTS if you guess the composition correctly. You can potentially earn a total of 100 POINTS, you lose 1 POINT for each drawing you make after the change has occurred (before you make your guess). If you make your guess before the urn has changed composition, you again lose 1 POINT for each draw by which your guess preceded the time the urn changed composition.
For example, if the change occurs just before the 8th draw, but you do not guess the composition until after the 15th draw, you lose 7 points. If you guessed after the 3rd draw, you lose 5 points. The points you accumulate will later be converted into money at the rate of 125 points to one dollar. At the end of the experiment, you will be paid what you have earned in all rounds, in cash.
The points earned in each group round will be awarded in its entirety to each member of the group. Since there are 20 rounds in total, and the maximum amount you can earn in each round is 80 cents, the theoretical maximum you could earn is $16.
